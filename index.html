Equipment Type and Age

Drive the Best

We take pride in our fleet. Our trucks are late models with automatic transmissions from:

  • 680 KW
  • Internationals
  • Cascadia

All of our equipment is under 2 years old and comes with APUs and 1,800 watt power inverters.

Trucks are governed at 67 MPH on the pedal and 65 MPH on cruise.

Minimum Qualification

Our goal is to always be straightforward with you during the interview process.

To drive for us, you must meet the following requirements:

Age: At least 22 years old

  • We will consider drivers that are 21 years old on a case-by-case basis

License: Valid & current CDL class A

Endorsements: None required

Experience: At least 1 year of CDL A driving experience

  • We will consider drivers with 9 months of CDL A experience at a single company with no traffic violations.

Driving record:

  • No DUI/DWI in the past 5 years
  • 3 or fewer moving violations in the past 3 years

Loads / Freight Hauled

Our loads are:

  • 100% dry van
  • 90% drop and hook
  • 99% no touch freight (paid lumpers for the 1%)
